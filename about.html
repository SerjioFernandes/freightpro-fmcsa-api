Our Story

CargoLume was born from a simple observation: the freight industry, despite moving trillions of dollars in goods annually, still relied on outdated methods of connecting shippers with carriers. Phone calls, spreadsheets, and manual processes dominated an industry ripe for innovation.

Our founding team, comprised of logistics veterans and technology innovators, set out to build a platform that would bring the freight industry into the modern era. We envisioned a world where finding the right carrier for your load was as simple as a few clicks, where real-time data and AI-powered algorithms would eliminate inefficiencies, and where transparency would be the standard, not the exception.

Today, CargoLume serves thousands of shippers and carriers across all 50 states, facilitating millions of dollars in freight transactions. But we're just getting started. Our commitment to innovation drives us to continuously improve our platform, add new features, and expand our capabilities to better serve the freight community.

Powered by Advanced Technology

AI-Powered Matching

Our proprietary AI algorithms analyze hundreds of data points to match loads with the most suitable carriers in seconds. Machine learning improves match quality over time, learning from successful shipments and user preferences.

Real-Time FMCSA Verification

Direct integration with the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration ensures all carriers are verified in real-time. We check DOT numbers, MC numbers, insurance status, and safety ratings instantly.

Market Intelligence

Access real-time rate data, market trends, and predictive analytics. Our platform analyzes millions of data points to provide accurate pricing recommendations and market insights.

Enterprise Security

Bank-level encryption, SOC 2 compliance, and multi-factor authentication protect your data. Regular security audits and penetration testing ensure your information stays safe.
